Prof Temitayo Akinbobola

Prof Temitayo Akinbobola is the COLLEGE OF ARTS MANAGEMENT AND SOCIAL SCIENCES. He is on the verge of completing his doctoral degree at the University of Ibadan, He has a multidisciplinary research interest, with particular focus in the fields of disaster management and significations, Ecolinguistics, Discourse Analysis, Pragmatics, Semiotics, Sociolinguistics, Literary, and Critical Stylistics, Social Psychology, Humour Studies, and Nigerian English, among others. He is a budding scholar and researcher, with a captivating research followership base on ResearchGate, Google Scholar, and Academia. He has published in notable, high-impact international and local journals. He has also made significant paper presentations at various conferences. He is a recipient of the Institut Français de Recherche en Afrique (French Institute for Research in Africa) Grant for Data Collection for COVID-19 Research, between April and September 2020.
